🤝 Mixed Doubles tips
• Agree on a verbal cue before the race (e.g. "Go!") — practise during sims
• Both partners run every 1km leg together — pace at the slower partner's speed
• Stronger partner goes first at: Sled Push, Sled Pull, Sandbag Lunges
• Faster mover goes first at: Burpee Broad Jumps
• Wall Balls: break into sets of 10–15, hand off on a clean completed rep
• Fumbled handoffs cost 5–15 sec per station — practise until it's automatic
What is Hyrox? ›
Hyrox is a global fitness race combining 8 km of running with 8 functional workout stations: Ski Erg, Sled Push, Sled Pull, Burpee Broad Jumps, Rowing, Farmer's Carry, Sandbag Lunges, and Wall Balls.

Tips for thyroid & PCOD athletes:
• Keep long runs at conversational pace (zone 2 — you can talk while running)
• Avoid training fasted for runs over 20 min — have a small snack first
• Prioritize 7–8 hrs sleep; thyroid recovery depends on it
• Hydrate well before and after every session
• Progress gradually — aim to improve 5 km time by 30 sec each week
• Listen to your body: fatigue that lingers 2+ days means recovery is needed
